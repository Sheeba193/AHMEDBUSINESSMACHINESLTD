Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I install the printer drivers?
You can download the latest printer drivers from our Downloads Page. Once downloaded, run the installer and follow the on-screen instructions.
How do I troubleshoot printing quality issues?
Make sure the ink cartridges are installed correctly and check the printhead for clogs. You can also run a cleaning cycle from the printer settings menu.
How do I connect my printer to Wi-Fi?
Press the Wi-Fi button on your Phantum printer and follow the setup instructions on your device. You'll need to select your Wi-Fi network and enter the password.

Troubleshooting Guide

Here are some common printer issues and how to fix them:

  • Printer not responding: Check if the printer is turned on and properly connected to your computer or network.
  • Paper jams: Turn off the printer and carefully remove any stuck paper. Avoid overloading the paper tray.
  • Low print quality: Ensure the ink cartridges are full and the printhead is clean.
  • Wireless connection issues: Restart your printer and router, then try reconnecting the printer to the Wi-Fi network.
